Chosen Quote

Mother Teresa (2013) Mother Teresa: 10 inspiring quotes – Where peace comes from, The Christian Science Monitor website, accessed 24 March 2026, Link.

Paragraph

Mother Teresa once said, "If we have no peace, it is because we have forgotten that we belong to each other." This idea reflects the core of SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals and its Target 17.16: Global Partnership for Sustainable Development, which emphasizes that global challenges can only be solved through cooperation. Today’s data shows how ignoring global interdependence can lead to economic and social consequences. Recent reports from the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs highlight slowing global economic growth and a $4 trillion annual gap in SDG financing, while around 800 million people remain in extreme poverty, all demonstrate that no country can succeed alone.

One practical solution is triangular partnerships:
  • Developing countries share proven technology-based solutions such as digital platforms and low-cost innovations and experience;
  • Least developed countries adapt these tools to their local infrastructure, workforce capacity, and environmental conditions;
  • Developed countries provide funding, advanced technologies like AI and satellite systems, as well as technical expertise to ensure effective implementation.

To take part in this idea, we suggest connecting with two international peers, hosting a short online discussion, and co-creating one simple, low-cost solution that can be implemented within a week to address a shared local challenge in the community.
